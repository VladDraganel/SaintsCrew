Gigs

Saints Crew was about community ever since its founding. If you want to see people come together as much as we want, join us in creating the best opportunities: concerts, music and street art contests, workshops and exhibitions. Whenever you have an idea, hit us up and we'll bring our whole team of multi-talented people to the table to make it happen!

Steps

We simplified the way in which we work so that it's easier for you to come with an idea!
See below how we roll:

1

Hit Us Up

Send us an e-mail and tell us what your idea is. 
Tell us when you want it to happen and how you would like for it to go down.
2

Making Plans

We'll get back to you and we can arrange a meeting to talk more and begin shaping the project.
After we inform you in what ways we can help you, we'll decide on the final details and we'll make plans for future meetings.
3

Getting Work Done

As soon as everything's decided, the whole Saints Crew will start working hard to ensure the event goes according to plan!
