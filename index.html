Our Story

In a nutshell, we're a band/art crew/event planning team in love with street art and culture.
It was when good music brought together graffiti crew "GVC" and a few skateboarding and bike-riding high school kids that Saints Crew was born. Driven by their love for street art and disappointed by the steady disappearance of young communities in their hometown, the group decided to bring their artistic flava to the public. Now, years later, Saints Crew is a solid art collective with numerous talented members, including MCs, beatmakers, DJs,  graphic artists and street writers. Our everyday activity as a crew includes artwork commissions, music production and event planning. See more details below!
