Street Writin'

Having grown up, we left the streets and the old walls and moved up to festivals, shop interiors, museums and art galleries across the country. Aside from our old friends from the GVC graffiti crew, we recruited a number of talented artists and writers to join our ranks, so that we can deliver the most outstanding pieces of street art in the area! Our skills have improved, but the style remains the same: Street-inspired. If you'd like to see some of our works up on your walls, or if you're an artist too, and you want to team up for an art project, hit us up so that we can begin sketchin' right away!
