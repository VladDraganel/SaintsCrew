MUSIC

We told you before: we love music. It's what we owe our existence as a collective to. Saints Crew has a numerous crowd of DJs, Beatmakers/Producers and MCs, so whether you'd like an instrumental for your own projects, or would like to propose a musical collab, we're ready to discuss!
We're die-hard fans of conscious Hip Hop and Reggae music, but we like to bring our own personal preferences when composing, therefore, expect afro-tribal/traditional Japanese/jazzy/folk influences! If you think we'd match, hit us up!
